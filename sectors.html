Sectors we serve

We specialise in hygiene-critical food manufacturing environments across New Zealand — where a verified clean isn't optional.

Our core specialty

Dairy

From processing and separation through drying, blending and packing, dairy plants run continuous, hygiene-critical operations. We clean and verify to keep your RMP intact and your lines running.

  • Processing & separation areas
  • Powder & dry-zone protocols
  • Tank, silo & CIP support
  • APC/micro verification

Food processing

RTE, bakery and prepared foods, including high-care line cleaning and changeover sanitation.

Meat

Boning rooms, processing and packing areas cleaned and sanitised to strict hygiene standards.

Beverage

Beverage production and bottling lines, including tank, vessel and CIP/COP cleaning support.

General manufacturing

Factory and manufacturing environments needing industrial deep cleaning and high-level work.
