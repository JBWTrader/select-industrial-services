Industrial hygiene cleaning, end to end

From daily production-line sanitation to planned shutdown deep cleans and high-level structural work — all delivered to a verified standard with factory-adapted tools.

Food-contact & production-line cleaning

Wet-clean and sanitation of lines, conveyors, fillers, slicers and food-contact surfaces using the 7-step best-practice sequence, finished with verification.

  • Daily, changeover and periodic cleaning
  • MPI-approved chemistry, correctly chosen and dosed
  • Pre-operational inspection & sign-off

High-care & high-risk area cleaning

Enhanced-hygiene cleaning of RTE and high-care zones with dedicated, segregated, colour-coded equipment and strict redline discipline.

  • Zone-dedicated tools that never cross the line
  • Gowning & access protocol adherence
  • Verified disinfection contact times

Tank, silo & CIP / COP

Internal cleaning of tanks, silos and closed systems, with clean-in-place and clean-out-of-place support for small parts and pipework.

  • Confined-space-aware procedures
  • Residue and allergen verification on request

High-level & structural cleaning

Work-at-height cleaning of structures, overhead services, ductwork and ceilings using scissor lifts and trained, WorkSafe-compliant operators.

  • Scissor-lift & height-access capability
  • Dust, condensate & overhead-soil control

Shutdown & periodic deep cleans

Planned shutdown, changeover and periodic deep-clean programmes scheduled around your production windows to minimise downtime.

  • Scoped, scheduled and resourced to your plan
  • Out-of-hours and weekend availability

Verification & audit support

APC & micro swabbing, ATP testing and clean documentation that keeps your RMP and HACCP records audit-ready between visits.

  • Result logging & trending
  • Pre-audit readiness checks

Cleanroom & controlled-environment cleaning

Cleaning of cleanrooms and controlled environments to ISO 14644 Class 7, 8 and 9 — including DNA-sensitive spaces with sensitive equipment and strict room requirements.

  • ISO Class 7 / 8 / 9 environments
  • DNA-sensitive contamination control

How every job runs

A consistent method, from first site visit to verified sign-off.

1

Site visit & risk assessment

We assess zones, hazards and requirements — documented before any cleaning commences.

2

Tailored cleaning plan

Method, chemistry, tools and zoning mapped to your plant and food-safety system.

3

Clean to standard

Trained crews execute the 7-step (or dry) protocol with segregated, factory-adapted tools.

4

Verify & document

APC/ATP verification, results recorded and trended — audit-ready every time.
