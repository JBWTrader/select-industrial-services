Hygiene specialists who prove the clean

Select Industrial Services is an Auckland-based industrial cleaning company focused on the one thing food and dairy manufacturers can't compromise on: verified hygiene.

Who we are

We clean hygiene-critical food manufacturing environments — and then we verify the result. That focus on evidence, not just appearance, is what keeps our clients audit-ready and their product safe.

Our work is built around your food-safety system. Every job starts with a documented risk assessment before cleaning commences, follows best-practice cleaning protocols with factory-adapted tools, and finishes with APC and micro verification recorded against your requirements.

We're Auckland-based and work with food, dairy, meat, beverage and general manufacturing plants across New Zealand. We also clean DNA-sensitive cleanrooms and controlled environments to ISO 14644 standards for reputable manufacturers such as Thermo Fisher Scientific and TechnoPlas NZ.

Safety-first on every site

We adhere strongly to your company's safety policy, risk matrix and procedures — and take a collaborative approach to WorkSafe NZ best practice on the floor.

JSA

Job Safety Analysis — we break each task into steps, identify the hazards and agree controls before work begins.

SLAM

Stop, Look, Assess, Manage — any crew member can pause a job the moment conditions change, then reassess before continuing.

Hierarchy of controls

Risk controlled in order of effectiveness — eliminate, substitute, isolate and engineer first; administration and PPE as the last lines of defence.

Four pillars of wellbeing

We support WorkSafe NZ's four pillars of workplace wellbeing — looking after the physical and mental health of our crews and yours.

Your site, your rules: we integrate with your safety policy, risk matrix, procedures, inductions and permit-to-work systems — and contribute to a positive safety culture while we're on site.
